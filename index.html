Top destinations

One of the most outstanding and most visited destinations in Tanzania is Zanzibar, it has it all from sandy beaches to pre and post colonial history.
Serengeti is undoubtedly one of the top wonders of the world. From wildebeest migration to lion prides it has it all, definetly it should be top three in your Tanzania bucketlist
Kilimanjaro is home to the roof of Africa Mount Kilimanjaro, it is the tallest free standing mountain in Africa. Kilimanjaro offers parks and hotspring for that well deserving natural warm bath
