About Tanzania and Beyond

Tanzania and beyond is all about exploring the beauty Tanzania has to offer through photography. from the beaches in Zanzibar to the roof of Africa(Mount Kilimanjaro). All these photos were taken with phone cameras while visiting various places within Tanzania. As we visit more places we will share with you some of interesting things we found along the way and we hope you will enjoy.
